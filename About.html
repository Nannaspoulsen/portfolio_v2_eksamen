My name is Nanna. I’ve always been fascinated by what it means to be human. That curiosity led me to philosophy. Then to therapy. Then to teaching. And now to design.

To me, these are not separate paths. They’re one long, winding journey toward clarity, expression and connection. As a multimedia design student with a background in psychotherapy, I aim to create from the inside out. I believe good design doesn’t just look good - it feels true. I still ask “why” more than most people. But now I use that question to shape meaning into form, and stories into systems. This portfolio is a reflection of where I’ve been and where I’m going.
